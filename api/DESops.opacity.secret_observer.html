Functions related to the secret observer construction for opacity

DESops.opacity.secret_observer.construct_secret_observer(g, h_ns, ns_state_sets, joint, obs_map)

Constructs the secret observer of the system, an automaton marking admissible observations

Here state markings represent behavior that we want to consider. Runs that do not end at marked states in both g and h_ns are not used as counterexamples or explanations for opacity. Most of the time this means that all states of g and h_ns should be marked. The secrecy of runs is defined entirely by ns_state_sets.

The marking of g_so on the other hand represents observations that violate opacity.

Parameters

  • g (Automata) – The automaton modeling the original system

  • h_ns (Automata) – The nonsecret specification automaton

  • ns_state_sets (sets: set) – Sets of states of h_ns corresponding to the different notions of nonsecrecy

  • joint (bool) – Whether or not to consider joint opacity (or separate)

  • obs_map – type obs_map: ObservationMap

Returns

The secret observer automaton
